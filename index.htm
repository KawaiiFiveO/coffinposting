Happy Halloween!
The long-awaited Halloween devlog.


Progress Report


Welcome back, you filthy animals. For Halloween, we will be giving away the game for free for a limited time. Simply claim it before the end of the day and it's yours to keep, forever!

It's been five months since the last update. As you may have guessed, progress on Episode 4A is still continuing steadily. Rest assured that we are still cooking and will not stop until the entire kitchen has burned down. There is no set release date yet, but we are planning tentatively for late 2026-early 2027.

Actually, you shouldn't trust us on that one. All we know for sure is that it will be released when it's done.

In the meantime, might we interest you in a game called In Stars and Time? Did you know they are releasing a Siffrin plushie that you can pre-order RIGHT NOW?

This, of course, is not foreshadowing the release of Andrew, Ashley, or Julia plushies. (But maybe merch will happen if there is enough demand. Not anytime soon, though.)

By the way, whoever comments on this post and correctly guesses the ending of the Burial route will receive 9 Kitpoints. These can be redeemed at the gachapon machine.

With that being said... see you next year!

Author's Remarks

I've been really into Silksong lately, so the development time might take a hit while I work on my Steel Soul runs. Thanks for understanding.
